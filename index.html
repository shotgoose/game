Dragon Age

Sword

Classic, sharp sword that does 30 physical damage per hit with no additional stack damage. It does not require any mana to use. Scales with strength.

Spark Spell

Scroll of a simple spell that does 20 electric damage and does 15 additional stack damage with a decline of 7 per turn. The spell has a chance to deal extra damage and costs 5 mana to use. Scales with intellect.

Magic Ring

Ancient magic ring that deals 10 damage per hit with 5 extra stack damage that has a decline of 3 per turn. The magic ring provides 5 extra max mana per level up. Scales with strength.
