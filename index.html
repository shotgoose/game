Dragon Slayer

Sword

Classic, sharp sword that does 30 physical damage per hit with no additional stack damage. It does not require any mana to use.

Spark Spell

Scroll of a simple spell that does 25 electric damage and does 15 additional stack damage with a decline of 7 per turn. The spell has a chance to deal extra damage and costs 7 mana to use.

Magic Ring

Ancient magic ring that deals 10 damage per hit with 5 extra stack damage that has a decline of 3 per turn. The magic ring provides 5 extra max mana per level up.
