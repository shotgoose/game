Sword

Classic, sharp sword that does 30 physical damage per hit with no additional stack damage. It does not require any mana to use. Scales with strength. Choosing this starter will grant you the brawler class, which is focused on using melee weapons to deal heavy damage to enemies at no cost and being able to absorb damage more easily from enemies.

Basic Scroll

Scroll of a simple spell that does 20 light damage. The spell also deals 10 stack damage that declines by 2 per turn. When hitting an enemy with this spell, your max mana has a 50% chance to increase by 2. Uses 7 mana. Scales with intellect. Choosing this starter will grant you the mage class, which is focused on using spells to deal more damage than possible with melee weapons, but takes mana and leaves the user with less armor and health.

Dagger

Extremely sharp dagger that deals 5 base damage and 10 bleed damage with a decline of 1 per turn. Bleed stack damage can keep stacking without having to reach 0. Scales with strength. Choosing this starter will grant you the assassin class, which is focused on performing takedowns on enemies before they can fight back, but if the takedown fails, surviving the fight will be difficult for an assassin.
